Ws Style & Semantics Element

<section> Element

The Section Elemnt represents a generic section of a document. The theme of each section should be identical, typically by including a heading (h1-h6 element) as a child of the section element.

A general rule is that the section element is a appropriate only if the element's content would be listed explicity in the document's outline

<article> Element

The article element represents a complete, or self contained, composition in a document, page, application, indepently distributable or reusuable.

This could be a forum post, a magazine or newspaper article, a blog entry a user subbmitted comment or any independent item of content.

When an article elements are nested, the inner article elements represents articles that are principle related to the contents of the outer article.
